Method

This forecast was predicted with the recency algorithm, a simpleprobably uselessmethod for determining stock prices. The recency algorithm predicts that the next price movement, \(M_{j}\), will be in the same direction as the most recent price movement. \(M_{i}\).

\[M_{i}= Close_{i} - Open_{i}\]

\[ M_{j}= \begin{cases} > 0, & \text{if } M_{i} > 0\\ \leq 0, & \text{if } M_{i}\leq 0 \end{cases} \]
